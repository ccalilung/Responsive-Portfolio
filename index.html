About Christian

Christian Calilung is an aspiring full-stack web developer focusing on data collection, query optimization, and data visualization. Currently, he works in clinical research as an IT coordinator where he executes clinical research data collection through web-based forms and surveys. Additionally, he leads the "data cleaning" effort where the collected data is reviewed for completeness, checked for accuracy, and formatted for analysis.

Christian earned his Bachelors of Science degree in Biochemistry and minors in Mathematics and Music from Boston College. He also holds a Masters of Science degree in Physiology and Biophysics from Georgetown University. In his spare time, Christian enjoys playing the piano, lifting weights, and going to bar trivia nights. A native of the Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex, Christian also closely follows his hometown's Mavericks, Rangers, Stars, and Cowboys.
